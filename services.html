Spatial Analysis

Once all the data has been collected, sorted, and checked for quality, the analysis can begin. Spatial data is a field that has really ballooned over the past 40 years. Although data collection is an important step being able to understand it becomes a large challenge. If you want help with making sense of your spatial data come to us. We have spatial analysts who have been specially trained at Fleming College and can handle all your analysis needs.
